How to learn

What is best for you?

Coding can be learnt in many forms. Most common is self-teach which involves hard work, dedication and discipline. You can have online courses or following online instructions. Some are even more adventerous but just troubleshoot until they achieve their aims. Others pay for teachers or companies to help. But the best and most useful is companies and organisations that teach for free. You get to meet a community that are in the same boat as you. You work with people who are actively doing the jobs or tasks that you aspire too.
